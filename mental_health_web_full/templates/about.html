About Mindful Chat

Supporting your mental wellness journey.

Our Mission

Mindful Chat provides a safe, private space to express your feelings and track your emotional wellbeing. We're not a replacement for professional help—we're a companion that helps you stay aware of your mood and reach out when needed.

How It Works

  • Chat — Share what's on your mind. Our system gently detects emotional tone and offers supportive responses.
  • Dashboard — View your mood over time and access all tools in one place.
  • Reminders — Schedule check-ins to build a habit of self-reflection.
  • Resources — Find crisis helplines and self-care tips when you need them.

Important

If you're experiencing a crisis or having thoughts of self-harm, please contact a mental health professional or crisis line immediately. See our Resources page for numbers and links.
