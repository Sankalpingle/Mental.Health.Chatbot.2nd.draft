Crisis Resources

If you're in crisis, please reach out. You don't have to face it alone.

🆘 24/7 Helplines

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (US): 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • International Association for Suicide Prevention: Find a center near you

Self-Care Tips

  • Practice deep breathing or grounding (5-4-3-2-1)
  • Reach out to a trusted friend or family member
  • Take a short walk or step outside
  • Limit social media and news if it's overwhelming
  • Rest when you need it—sleep affects mood

When to Seek Professional Help

Consider talking to a therapist or counselor if you feel persistently sad, anxious, or overwhelmed, or if daily life feels difficult to manage. Professional support can make a real difference.
